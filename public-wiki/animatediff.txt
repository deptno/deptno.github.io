<li><code class="span">Video Combine VHS</code> -&gt; 영상생성</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><code class="span">VAE Decoder</code> -&gt; <code class="span">Video Combine VHS</code></li>
<li><code class="span">Empty Latent Image</code> batch size 를 16(frame 수만큼)으로 설정</li>
</ul>
