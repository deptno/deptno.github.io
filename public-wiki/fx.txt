<pre><code class="hljs language-sh"><span class="hljs-built_in">echo</span> <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;{&quot;name&quot;: &quot;world&quot;}&#x27;</span> | fx <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;x =&gt; x.name&#x27;</span> <span class="hljs-string">&#x27;x =&gt; `Hello, ${x}!`&#x27;</span> <span class="hljs-comment"># javascript 함수 지원</span>
